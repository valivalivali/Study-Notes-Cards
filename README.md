# Study Notes & Cards - Support

Welcome to the official support page for Study Notes & Cards!

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. Where is my data stored?**  
All your Subjects, Decks, Notes, and Cards are stored 100% locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not use any servers, and we never have access to your study materials or personal data. Everything belongs to you.

**2. How does the grading system work?**  
During a Study Session, you can grade your recall (Again, Hard, Good, Easy). The app uses a spaced repetition algorithm to determine exactly when you should see each card again to maximize learning and retention.

**3. The app is freezing or behaving unexpectedly. What should I do?**  
Please ensure you are updated to the latest version of macOS or iOS. If the issue persists, trying completely closing the app and restarting it.
